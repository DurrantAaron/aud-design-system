The rule
Neutrals never change. Ground, ink, type and layout are identical across every app — that’s what makes them read as one suite. Only the accent moves.
Why warm
The off-white ground (#F2F0EB) is the same warm paper Anthropic uses. Every accent is pulled toward that warmth and desaturated, so nothing screams or clashes against it.
Colour logic
Brass = the anchor / flagship. Clay = care & first aid (warm red without the alarm). Steel = night-shift control. Sage = operations overview. Eucalypt = cleaning, the most Australian note in the set.
Accent discipline
Accent is for the “u”, active states, key rules and the one thing that matters per screen — never large fills. Restraint is the military-grade signal.
